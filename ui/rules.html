Lockpick Lab Rules

Deterministic, single-player puzzles. No randomness, no hidden information.

Core Objective

  • Reduce the boss health to 0 within the target number of rounds.
  • The boss does not take turns; it is a fixed puzzle state.
  • All information is visible: hands, boards, and effects.

Setup

  • Boss health and target rounds are set by the puzzle.
  • You start with a fixed amount of mana and a defined hand.
  • Mana per round is fixed by the puzzle and carries over.
  • Enemy creatures may already be on the board.

Zones

  • Player board: your creatures in play.
  • Enemy board: boss creatures in play.
  • Hand: cards available to play this turn.

Resources

  • Mana pays the cost of playing cards.
  • Mana carries over between rounds.
  • Power is the single combat stat. It is both damage and resilience.

Card Types

  • Creatures enter the board and can attack once per round.
  • Spells resolve instantly and then leave play.
  • Effects stay on the battlefield and modify rules (ex: War Banner adds +1 power while attacking).
  • Mods attach to any creature (player or boss) and permanently modify it.

Actions & Turn Flow

  • You may take any number of actions in any order until you end the round.
  • Creatures can attack once per round, then become tired.
  • At end of round, all tired creatures become rested.
  • Mana refills according to the puzzle setup.

Combat Rules

  • If the boss has any creatures on the board, you must attack a creature.
  • If there are no enemy creatures, you may attack the boss directly.
  • When a creature attacks another creature, both deal their power simultaneously.
  • Any creature reduced to 0 power is destroyed.

Spells

Blightwave

Deals 2 damage to all creatures on both sides.

Toxic Mist

Gives all your creatures Venom.

Cleanse

Removes all mods from a creature.

Brood Herald

When played, it summons a Broodling for the boss.

Keywords & Mechanics

Guard

While a Guard creature exists, you must attack a Guard first.

Storm

Can attack any target immediately on the turn it is played.

Pierce

Excess power from a creature attack spills over to the boss.

Testudo

If flanked by friendly creatures, this creature takes no combat damage.

Wooden Shield

Mods a creature for -1 power and blocks the next damage it would take, then breaks.

Vigil Banner

At end of round, your untired creatures gain +1 power.

Bone Requiem

At end of a round where 4+ creatures died, copy the strongest boss creature to your board.

Death Ward

Whenever a creature dies, gain a counter. At 5 counters, you may activate it to deal 3 damage to any target.

Venom

When a creature with Venom attacks, it gives the target a poison token.

Poison

Each poison token deals 1 damage at the end of every round.

Brood

When a creature with Brood is damaged but survives, it spawns a Broodling (1 power) next to it.

Scavenger

Gains +1 power whenever another creature dies.

Rebirth

When this creature dies, it returns with +1 power.

Relay

When this creature attacks another creature, adjacent allies gain power equal to the damage dealt.

Requiem Rune

When a creature with this mod dies, it deals 2 damage to the boss.

Flank Rune

At the end of each round, the creatures immediately to the left and right gain +1 power.

Gravecaller

When this creature dies, it deals 2 damage to all enemy creatures.

Order

Can only be played if you have an untired creature; when played, all your creatures become tired.

Sleepy

Enters the board already tired.

Chain

Triggers a bonus effect if you have already played a card this round.

Sacrifice

Destroy a friendly creature to trigger an effect.

Determinism

  • No randomness. No hidden information. Every puzzle is fully knowable.
  • Identical actions always result in identical outcomes.
